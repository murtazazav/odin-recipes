Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

Here's a delicious recipe for chocolate chip cookies that I can share with you! The resulting cookies will be thick and beautiful, with crispy edges and a gooey centre that everyone will love. Perfect for a quick and tasty treat any day of the week!

Ingredients

A list of the ingredients required for this recipe:

  • 225g (1 cup) caster sugar
  • 300g (2 cups) plain flour (sifted)
  • 200g (1 cup) melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 100g-200g chocolate chips

Steps

Follow these to end up with some delicious cookies!

  1. Add sugar + melted butter in a bowl and mix
  2. Add vanilla extract to the sugary butter mixture and mix
  3. Sift flour + baking powder + salt into a second bowl and mix
  4. Join the dry and wet mixtures together and mix with hands or spatula
  5. Add the egg and mix again
  6. Mix in the chocolate chips
  7. If the mixture feels a little too soft at this point, then place in the fridge for about 15-20 mins
  8. Place some baking paper onto a tray
  9. Roll the mixture into balls and place onto the tray leaving gaps in between each ball so they don't join together
  10. Bake at 160c for 15-20 mins
Enjoy the delicious cookies!
