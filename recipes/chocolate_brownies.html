Rich Chocolate Brownies Recipe

Description

Indulge in the most decadent and mouth-watering chocolate brownies you've ever tasted with this incredible recipe. Get ready to experience the perfect balance of chewy and gooey goodness that will leave everyone wanting more! Discover the essential ingredients and step by step instructions that make these brownies unforgettable.

Ingredients

A list of the ingredients required for this recipe:

  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/8 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs (large)
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 500g dark chocolate (melted)
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

Follow these to end up with some delicious brownies!

  1. Preheat the oven at 160c
  2. Grease a baking tray and line it with baking paper
  3. Whisk butter and sugar together
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla to the mix and whisk again
  5. Sift in the flour and salt and whisk
  6. Add the melted chocolate and whisk
  7. Pour the mixture into the baking tray and level it out
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes
